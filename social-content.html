30-Day Social Content Calendar

Content pillars, platform strategy, and day-by-day posting plan to drive quiz traffic and brand engagement

Content Pillars

All content ladders back to the quiz funnel as the primary conversion path. Four pillars rotate throughout the month to keep content fresh and cover all audience segments.

🎨

Behind the Seams

Artist spotlights, painting process, studio life, before/after transformations

📚

Style Education

Handbag care, styling tips, trend reports, luxury market insights

⭐

Social Proof

Customer reviews, celebrity features, press mentions, unboxing reactions

❓

Quiz Promotion

Profile reveals, quiz results, personality content, direct quiz CTAs

Platform Strategy

Instagram (Primary)

100K+ followers. Primary driver of quiz traffic. Mix of feed, Stories, and Reels.

  • Feed: 3-4 posts/week (high-quality product imagery, carousels)
  • Stories: Daily (polls, Q&As, behind-the-scenes, quiz link stickers)
  • Reels: 2-3/week (painting process, transformations, profile reveals)
  • Bio link: Quiz landing page as primary destination

TikTok (Growth)

High discovery potential for younger audience (Aspiring Luxurists). Video-first.

  • Frequency: 3-5 videos/week
  • Format: 15-60 sec painting clips, reaction videos, "did you know" facts
  • Hooks: "Watch me paint over a Louis Vuitton" / "POV: your bag is a masterpiece"
  • CTA: "Link in bio to find your handbag personality"

Pinterest (Discovery)

Visual search platform. Fashion content has long shelf life. High purchase intent users.

  • Frequency: 5-10 pins/week
  • Format: Product photography, "style inspiration" boards, infographics
  • Boards: "Hand-Painted LV," "Luxury Gift Ideas," "Handbag Style Quiz"
  • CTA: All pins link to quiz landing page
