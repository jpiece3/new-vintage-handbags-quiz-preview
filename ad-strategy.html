Facebook / Instagram Ads

Cold Audience Hooks (Awareness)

For people who have never heard of New Vintage Handbags. Goal: spark curiosity and drive quiz completions.

Identity

"Your bag says more about you than you think."

Take our 90-second Handbag Personality Quiz and find out if you're a Statement Maker, Classic Collector, Creative Rebel, Sentimental Gifter, or Aspiring Luxurist. Your results include personalized picks from our hand-painted Louis Vuitton collection.

Art + Fashion

"What if your Louis Vuitton was also a painting?"

Every bag in our collection is an authentic, pre-owned LV that's been hand-painted into a one-of-a-kind masterpiece. Take the quiz to find the style that was literally made for you. (Yes, celebrities love them too.)

Social Proof

"Miranda Lambert carries one. Megan Fox carries one. What's your style?"

The same artists who paint for A-listers will create your piece too. Take the 90-second Handbag Personality Quiz and discover which hand-painted style matches your vibe. Over 10,000 bags painted since 2016.

Warm Audience Hooks (Retargeting)

For website visitors, Instagram engagers, and quiz abandoners. Goal: bring them back to complete the quiz.

FOMO

"That bag you were looking at? It's one-of-a-kind."

Every painted bag in our collection exists exactly once. Take the Handbag Personality Quiz and we'll match you with the pieces that fit your style before someone else gets there first.

Smart Luxury

"Tired of paying more for the same bag everyone carries?"

Luxury houses raise prices. Quality declines. Meanwhile, our artists turn authentic pre-loved LV bags into wearable art that nobody else owns. Take the quiz. Find your one-of-one.

Curiosity

"We know your handbag personality. Do you?"

Statement Maker? Classic Collector? Creative Rebel? 90 seconds and 7 fun questions stand between you and your personalized style profile. Plus curated picks from our hand-painted collection.

Hot Audience Hooks (Conversion)

For quiz completers who haven't purchased, email subscribers, and high-intent engagers. Goal: drive product page visits and custom requests.

Gift Angle

"The gift that makes everyone cry (happy tears)."

Hand-painted pet portrait pouches, custom designed bags, and one-of-a-kind accessories. Give someone the most unforgettable gift they'll ever carry. Starting with our hand-painted pouches.

Urgency

"Your quiz picks are still available. For now."

You took the quiz and we matched you with your perfect hand-painted style. Those pieces are one-of-a-kind and they don't last long. See if yours is still available.

Custom

"If you can dream it, our artists can paint it."

Already know what you want? Our custom request process is completely collaborative. You'll get a digital mock-up before we touch a brush. Your vision. Your bag. Your masterpiece.
