A complete guide to your new quiz funnel: what it is, how it works, why it drives results, and everything included in your build.

1. What This Is

Your "What's Your Handbag Personality?" quiz funnel is a fully automated lead generation and nurture system built specifically for New Vintage Handbags.

When someone lands on the quiz page, they answer 7 quick questions about their style preferences and handbag personality. Based on their answers, they're matched with one of 5 personality profiles and receive personalized product recommendations from your hand-painted collection.

After submitting their email, they enter an automated email sequence (26 emails total) tailored to their profile, their quiz answers, and their buying readiness. Hot leads get direct CTAs. Cold leads get education. Everyone gets value.

3. Why It Works

Psychology-Driven Conversion

  • Identity Labeling: Giving users a personality name ("You're The Creative Rebel") creates an aspirational identity they want to act on
  • Real Scarcity: Every NVH bag is genuinely one-of-a-kind. The urgency emails reference real scarcity, not manufactured countdowns
  • Three-Layer Personalization: Temperature (buying readiness) + Profile (personality type) + Quiz Answers (specific responses) create emails that feel genuinely personal
  • Risk Reversal: The digital mock-up process is featured prominently. "See your bag before we paint it" eliminates the biggest purchase barrier
  • Progressive Value: Results page delivers value first (style insights, blindspot reveal) before any CTA. Emails educate before they sell
  • Social Proof Stacking: Celebrity clients, 200+ reviews, press features, and specific customer testimonials deployed across the entire funnel
  • Anti-Bagflation Angle: Taps into real consumer frustration with luxury price hikes and declining quality. Positions NVH as the smarter choice
